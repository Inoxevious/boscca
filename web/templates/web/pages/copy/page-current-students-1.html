Current Students

As a members, it's all about having the right information at the right time. You need to know how to get information when you need it—whether it’s tips on how to study, important dates or access to your members records. And it's not all about the classroom—making time for other activities on Community, such as fitness and clubs, is just as important.

If you can't find the information you need, we're here to help! Ask us questions like:

  • When is the exam timetable posted?
  • How long will it take for my members loan application to be assessed?
